Questionnaire review tool for Disability Data & Research

{#
#} {#
#} {#
#}

The questionnaire review tool for disability data & research (QR-DDR) allows users to identify and categorize questions on disability in selected documents. It was developed with a view to identify relevant questions on disability in the questionnaires of surveys and censuses as part of the Disability Data Initiative, enabling reviewers to analyze documents in a faster and more reliable way.

The tool is the result of a collaboration between the Data Science for Digital Health (DS4DH) group based at the University of Geneva and the Disability Data Initiative (DDI) based at Fordham University.

Please note that this tool does not substitute for searching manually through a document but may help with the process. Guidance on how to use this tool is available at this link.
